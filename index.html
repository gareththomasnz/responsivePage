Conspiracy Booster Box

Pick. Plot. Play! Experience a Magic format where the intrigues begin long before the first spells are cast! Revolutionary new abilities impact every part of the play experience, starting with the draft itself. Do you have the ruthlessness and guile to prevail?

Gameplay

Unlike normal drafts, where players pass around boosters pulling out the card that they want each time, Conspiracy has drafts of six to eight players who split up into two groups for a free-for-all. Card mechanics will deal primarily with the adjusted format of draft, such as Cogwork Librarian's effect, which allows a player to exchange it for a different drafted card. A new card type, Conspiracy, will be introduced. A new Planeswalker, Dack Fayden, will be introduced, marking the first time he's on a card, after appearing in a 2011 comic book. Multiple cards from Conspiracy are in the Vintage Masters expansion for Magic Online, including cards with the Conspiracy-themed mechanics Will of the Council, Parley, and Dethrone.

Additional Information

Conspiracy features 210 black-bordered cards, including randomly inserted premium versions of all cards in the set. There are sixty-five new cards in Conspiracy. Fifty-two of the new cards will be legal in Legacy and Vintage; thirteen cards will not.
